Google Forms Quiz Builder

Designed a streamlined quiz authoring workflow to reduce manual setup time and improve consistency in form-based assessment creation.

Problem

Manual quiz setup was repetitive and error-prone, making it difficult to maintain consistent question quality and structure.

Architecture

  • Modular front-end workflow for question creation and ordering.
  • Reusable form structures to support consistent output.
  • Data handling patterns optimized for predictable quiz generation.

Tradeoffs

Focused on dependable authoring UX and maintainable logic first; deferred advanced analytics and larger integration scope for later phases.

Outcomes

  • Reduced friction in creating and organizing assessments.
  • Improved consistency in quiz formatting and structure.
  • Established a cleaner foundation for future automation work.
